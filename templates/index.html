Welcome to the Johari Window Generator

An app to help improve self-awareness within your community of friends, family, colleagues, teams, etc.


Don't have an account? Sign up here.

Were you given a key by an existing user? Submit data here.

Instructions

Step 1. Create a username/password and then choose a key to share with others so they can find you. Then you'll select adjectives from a list that YOU feel describe YOURSELF.

Step 2. Share your username and shareable key with others so they can select adjectives from the same list that THEY feel describe YOU.

Step 3. Login to your profile to see your Johari Window. The window will show the adjectives that:
  • You both said describe you.
  • Only they (and not you) chose describe you.
  • Only you (and no one else) chose to describe you.
  • Neither you nor others chose to describe you.
  The app will show you more about what you're putting out there for others to see.
